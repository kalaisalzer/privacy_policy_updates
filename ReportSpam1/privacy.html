​Notice to California Residents

Notice of Collection of Personal Information:  We list the categories of Personal Information that we collect under “Information Collection.”  We collect Personal Information about you for business and commercial purposes as described under “Information Use.”  For more information, including on how to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information, please see “Your Rights” below.

 

 

INFORMATION COLLECTION

​When information identifies or is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with you, we refer to it as “Personal Information.” The personal information we collect includes the following categories:

  • Identifiers/Contact information: your name, email address, phone number, or any other information that you choose to provide in the content of the message.
  • Internet or other Electronic Network Activity Information: We may collect information related to the message that you have reported including your mobile carrier or email service provider, the date and time of the message received, and information regarding your interactions with the SpamResponse Service.
  • Content of Communication: We may collect contents of reported communications including the telephone number or email address of the sender and the content of the spam message.
  • Geolocation Data: We may collect data about the location of the device you use to access our site or Services.
  • Professional or Employment-related Information: We may collect the name of your company if you choose to provide it in the content of a message.

 

INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

Personal Information You Provide When You Interact with SpamResponse: Since our Services are intended to help prevent unwanted spam, we try to limit our collection of information through the Services. We will collect information from you on the Services when you choose to share it with us. This may include when you request information from us, submit a spam report, or otherwise choose to contact us. If you decline to provide any of the requested or required information, you may not be able to take full advantage of the Services and their features.

Personal Information You Provide about a Third Party: In reporting receipt of an unsolicited message, you may share with us information about another person including the phone number and email address of the sender. You may also authorize our mobile application to access your contacts in order to filter messages that you received from your contents and to distinguish those messages from spam messages. You may disable this access at any time.

INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED

When you visit or interact with the SpamResponse site or Services, we, as well as third party advertisers and/or service providers, may use a variety of technologies that automatically or passively collect information about your online activity.

  • Device Information: We may automatically collect unique identifiers for the computer, mobile device, tablet, or other device (collectively, “Device”) you use to access the SpamResponse Service, including hardware model and mobile network information.
  • Message Information: When you report receipt of an unsolicited message, such as a spam you are receiving (email or text), we automatically collect the message content, network provider, sender number, message time, location, and device type.
  • Usage Information: We may use cookies or other technologies to automatically or passively collect certain information from and about you when you use our Services. To learn more about the cookies used on the Services, and ways to limit certain tracking or to decline cookies, please visit WMC Global’s Privacy Policy.
  • Third Party Service Providers: We may use third party service providers to support the SpamResponse Service. Some of these service providers may use technology such as cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, log files, or other technologies to receive, collect, and store Personal Information on our behalf.
  • Third Party Analytic Technologies: We may use third parties’ analytics and tracking tools, such as Google Analytics, to help us track, segment, and analyze usage of the SpamResponse Service.  These tools may use technology such as cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, log files, Flash cookies, or other technologies to track online activity. They may also combine information they collect from your interaction with the SpamResponse Service with information they collect from other sources. We may not have access to information these third parties collect using cookies or other tracking technologies.

California Do Not Track Disclosures: Our system may not respond to do not track requests or headers from some or all internet browsers.
